Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Corgis are affectionate, cute, and friendly dogs that have strong athletic capabilities. They weigh 27 to 30 pounds and come in a range of colors from red, sable, fawn, black, tan, and white.

  1. Family life
    • Good with children
    • Affectionate with family
    • Good with other dogs
  2. Physical
    • Shedding level high
    • Low drooling level
    • Coat grooming moderate
  3. Social
    • Open to strangers
    • Very playful
    • Very protective

Corgi Care

Health Grooming Exercise
Hip evaluation, ophthalmologist evaluation Daily combover, regular nail trimming Moderate daily exercise, long walks

Dobermans

The Dobermann or also known as the Doberman Pinscher is a medium to large sized dog that was discovered by german a tax collector Louis Dobermann in the 1890s. Dobermans are both sleek and powerful, possessing keen intelligence and a striking physique.They are considered to be royalty in the canaine communtiy and are among the finest protection dogs.They are known to be intellegent, alert and extremely loyal.

Size and Porportions

The size of the breeds varies through kennles and breeding clubs,but according to standards males are approximately 68 to 72 centimenters(27-28 inch) at the withers. According females standards should be around 63 to 68 centimenters(25-27 inchs).This dog has a more square frame so the length should equal the height. The length of head, neck and legs should be porportionate to the body.

Weight

The ideal weight for male dogs is around 88-99 pounds and for females the ideal weight is around 71-77 pounds.

Fun Fact: Dobermans were the first canine war heroes

Pug

Pugs are charming, loving, and mischievous dogs that live to love and to be loved. They weigh 14 to 18 pounds and come in the colors of fawn and black.

  1. Family life
    • Good with children
    • Affectionate with family
    • Alright with other dogs
  2. Physical
    • Shedding level somewhat high
    • Low drooling level
    • Coat grooming moderate
  3. Social
    • Open to strangers
    • Very playful
    • Somewhat protective

German Shepherd

German Shepherds are Smart, Loyal, and Courageous dogs. They are all purpose workers that will protect the family it belongs to. They can stand as high as 26 inches tall and can come in many different fur colors, the most common being black and tan.

  1. Family life
    • Very affectionate with family
    • Very good with children
    • Moderately good with other dogs
  2. Physical
    • Shed quite a lot
    • Only need to groom every few days to ever week
    • Not very likely to drool
  3. Social
    • Moderately open to strangers
    • Very playful but need rest peroids
    • Very vigilant watchdogs and guard dogs
    • Highly Adaptable
  4. Personality
    • Very easy to train
    • Very high energy
    • Moderately vocal when it comes to barking
    • Needs to be doing a job or activity

German Shepherd Care

Health Grooming Exercise
Hip Evalution, Elbow Evalution Brush coat every few days, monthly nail trimming, occasional bath Daily long walks, frequent playtime
