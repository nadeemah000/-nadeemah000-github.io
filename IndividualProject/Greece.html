Stories of Ancient Greece




Greek mythology consists a body of stories, myths and rituals concerning the greek gods and heros. Greek mythology had a great influence on art and literature of western civilization. Many countries and other civilizations have also developed stories that explain the existenece of worldy phenomenons. The Greeks myths have been unrivaled in the western world for imagination and more captivating ideas. Artists and poets from prehistoric times have aquired the inspiration from Greek mythology and have found relevence in classical mythical themes.
